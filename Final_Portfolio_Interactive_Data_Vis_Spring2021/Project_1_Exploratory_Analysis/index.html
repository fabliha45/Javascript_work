Deforestation

Deforestation is the decrease in forestland across the world that are lost for different uses, for example, rural croplands, urbanization, mining exercises and on occasion for natural causes. Due to the rapid development of our industry, lifestyles and human activities, there has been a negative affect on our ecosystem with high deforestation rate. Deforestation can largely cause climate change, soil erosion, desertification, flooding, decrease in crops grown and most importantly surge in greenhouse gases which is one of the most essential cause of Global Warming.

In about every second there is about one and half acres of forest land cut down. If deforestation is not controlled, then in about 100 years there will not be any rainforests on our planet anymore. One of the major components, our water cycle is strongly affected by deforestation.
